✈️ General Travel Tips

  • 📱 Save digital copies of your passport & visa to a secure cloud drive before departure.
  • 📶 Buy an international e-SIM (e.g. Airalo, Holafly) before landing — instant connectivity, no roaming fees.
  • 🔌 Carry a universal adapter with multiple USB-C ports — one charger for all devices.

₿ Bitcoin Travel Tips

  • ⚡ Use peer-to-peer Lightning apps (Phoenix, Breez) to spend sats globally — avoid bank forex fees entirely.
  • 💾 Keep a hardware wallet (Trezor/Coldcard) in your carry-on, never checked luggage.
  • 🛡️ Encrypt a paper backup of your seed phrase and store it separately from your wallet.
